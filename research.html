Research Interests

My research interests focuses on the transformative role of artificial intelligence in driving innovation and enhancing business strategy. I am particularly interested in exploring how AI technologies can revolutionize industries by improving customer experiences, optimizing risk management, and enabling product development.

My ongoing research paper delves into how strategic intent translate into AI implementation to lead organizations to sustainable success. This work aims to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and their practical applications in solving real-world business challenges.

I actively engage with industry leaders and participate in conferences to deepen my understanding of AI's potential and its influence across various sectors.
